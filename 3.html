ALL ABOUT HOVER

This website has hover effects going on everywhere. The overall design looks really simple and minimal. Typefaces and typography are treated nicely using different types of typefaces, still having clean design. But it draws audience's attention more has the hover effects come to the place. all about parallax

Parallax scrolling really interests audience. That's the reason why many websites use parallax. It could be very distracting or too loud depending on how much animation or color you have. However this website well managed to make the design very crisp and solid by just using black and white. Everything staying in the certain grid, text placements were very stable even though they look like they're floating around.







all about parallax 2

Colors are very bright. It could've been very loud if this website relies on graphic design. There are some risks to take and I've seen very few designers who could fully bring up their potentials using various color palettes. However, this website is more about animation, not graphic design. Colors are harmonious and makes the website very strong. The website also brings up the potential parallax scrolling has. It shows the story that animation has and let's audience concentrate on the performance shown on screen.







all about parallax 3

Optical illusion makes the background three-dimensional. The distinct division of foreground and background, and the use of parallax effect makes you move the image around separately with horizontal scrolls. This is another step that many designers can take to develop their websites further. Whatever the photographs are, if it has foreground and background, they can use them, change them to three dimensional space to make them more sophisticated.

all about smoothness

This website requires vertical scrolling throughout the page like any other websites. The main page starts with a slideshow on top, and their logo and navigation bar on the bottom. However, as you scroll down, you can see the navigation bar sticks on top smoothly so it stays fixed on the window. It moves down again when you scroll up till the slideshow appears. This effect gives designers more various range of designing the web, since they can place the navigation bar anywhere else then just the top.
